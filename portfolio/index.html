Las Vegas, NV

I have been living in the city of Las Vegas for about 8 years now. Within Las Vegas, I enjoy visiting Lake Mead and going boating while also taking time to visit the mountains that surround Las Vegas and taking on the various hikes that are available year-round.

Hiking and Camping

When I am not coding, I enjoy going hiking and camping. One of my favorite places to hike is in Zion, Utah. While there, I always make sure to hike Angel's Landing first thing in the morning. The view from the top of Angel's Landing is easily one of the most breath-taking views that you could ever imagine.

Coffee

I am a huge coffee snob and love craft coffees from local mom-and-pop coffee shops. My favorite way to drink coffee is the pourover method. Currently, I have been drinking an East Timor single origin from a local coffee roastery in Las Vegas, Nevada.
